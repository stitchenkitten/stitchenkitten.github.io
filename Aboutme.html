Meet the Designer

I decided to move to New York City 2 years ago and I love every minute of it. Meeting new people expanding my horizons the usual small town girl in a big city romance story. Of course I also adopted my first cat.

I have always enjoyed the company of animals from living with them to volunteering at animal shelters. When I went to school for Apparel Merchandising and Design I asked my Professor if I could make a line of pet clothes. I was given a straight up NO and so I squandered the idea.

When it was way to cold to go out and experience the NYC lifestyle, I picked up my knitting needles and just made something for my cat. Just a simple knitted collar. However, I just loved how she looked in it and I posted a picture and I was given great feedback. I thought what else can I do? So I made a Bow Tie, and then a Tie. Some people at work saw it and also wanted one, so I thought I can make something out of this.

I finally got to utilize the skills I learned from college to something I fee really passionate about. I love pulling my ideas from the city, comics, movies, and books. I'm just your everyday knitting crazy cat lady.
